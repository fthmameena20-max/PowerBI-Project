🏥 Healthcare Operations Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **Healthcare Operations Dashboard** is developed using Power BI to analyze and visualize hospital data, including patients, doctors, treatments, appointments, and billing. It provides a comprehensive view of hospital performance and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor total revenue and billing status
* Analyze patient distribution and doctor performance
* Identify high-performing treatments
* Track monthly trends in revenue

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** 551.25K
* **Total Patients:** 50

---

## 📈 Visualizations

* **Bar Chart:** Average revenue by treatment type
* **Line Chart:** Monthly revenue trend
* **Bar Chart:** Patient count by doctor
* **Pie Chart:** Payment status distribution (Paid, Pending, Failed)
* **Gauge Chart:** Cost analysis (average, min, max)
* **Treemap:** Revenue contribution by treatment and gender
* **Slicer:** Doctor-wise filtering

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* MRI and Physiotherapy generate the highest revenue
* Revenue fluctuates across different months
* Some payments remain pending or failed
* Doctor performance varies based on patient count

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard delivers a **clear, interactive, and insightful view of healthcare operations**, helping stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and improve decision-making.
